Gross Oatmeal

A delicious breakfast delight made of oats and topped with brown sugar

My momma used to make this recipes during the dark dark nights of the first clone war. I remember feeling comfort despite the faint blaster fire heard in the distance when consuming this masterpiece.

Basically, you just put the oats in a pot with some water and let them absorb some water for 10 minutes or something. Then you turn on the heat until the water is at a boil and let the oats cook until they are a consistency that you like. Then you put it in a bowl and put whatever you want on top like some fruit or brown sugar, and pray the clones don't make it to your home.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Water
  • Brown Sugar

Ordered Steps

  1. Put oats in the pot with water. Water level should be higher than oats.
  2. Let oats soak in water for around 10 minutes
  3. Turn the heat on and let the oats cook until they are the consistency you like
  4. Bowl the oatmeal and add brown sugar or whatever other topping you'd like to the top
