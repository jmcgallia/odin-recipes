Chocolate Milk

Chocolate Milk - Teach Children it's okay to add sugar to everything at an early age!

A classic chocolate milk recipe passed down through the ages, said to have been written by Aritosthenes himself, soon after accurately calculating the circumference of the world using sticks in the sand in ancient Egypt.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Syrup
  • Milk

Ordered Steps

  1. Pour the milk into a cup
  2. Pour some chocolate syrup into the milk which is in the cup
  3. Stir the milk and chocolate syrup until consistent
  4. Drink Drink Drink
