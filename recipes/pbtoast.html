Toast with peanut butter and bananas on it

A quick, healthy, and delicious breakfast

Ever feel the need to deprive yourself of the basic joys of a decent breakfast because you're in a hurry to go to work and be taken advantage of by a boss who doesn't appreciate or value you and pockets 99% of the value that you create?

If so, then we have the perfect solution for you! We've got peanut butter, on a piece of bread. And then some sliced bananas on top. You could also add honey if you want but that's a little bit expensive isn't it?

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Bread
  • Banana
  • Honey (optional)

Ordered Steps

  1. Toast the bread (if you're feeling really fancy today)
  2. Put a piece of bread on a plate
  3. Spread peanut butter on top of that piece of bread
  4. Optionally, drizzle or spread some honey on top of the peanut butter
  5. Slice a banana and lay out the slices on top of the peanut butter
  6. CONSUME CONSUME CONSUME CONSUME
